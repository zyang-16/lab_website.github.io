The Future of Agentic AI: From Theory to Practice

Abstract

Recent advances in Large Language Models (LLMs) have paved the way for autonomous agents capable of complex reasoning and tool use. However, significant challenges remain in reliability, safety, and multi-agent collaboration.

In this seminar, we will discuss the scaling laws of agent capabilities and demonstrate our latest framework, AgentBeats, which achieves state-of-the-art performance on coding benchmarks.

Speaker Bio

Prof. Jie Tang
Professor, Tsinghua University

Jie Tang is a Professor at the Department of Computer Science and Technology, Tsinghua University. His research interests include artificial general intelligence, data mining, and social networks.

Weekly Seminar: Multimodal Reasoning in ChatGLM

Abstract

We present the architecture behind the latest ChatGLM multimodal capabilities, focusing on how visual encoders are aligned with the language model backbone.

The talk covers data collection strategies, alignment training, and evaluation results on standard VQA benchmarks.
