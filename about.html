About @ Tsinghua KEG

Bridging Knowledge and Intelligence for a Better Future.

Our Vision

"To compute knowledge for artificial general intelligence and empower global academic discovery."

At Tsinghua KEG (Knowledge Engineering Group), we believe that the next generation of AI must bridge the gap between data-driven learning and symbolic knowledge. Our vision is to build intelligent systems that can understand, reason, and generate knowledge at a human level.

History & Milestones

Founded in 1996, KEG has evolved from a small research unit into a global hub for AI innovation. Here are some key moments in our journey.

1996

KEG Established

The Knowledge Engineering Group was founded within the Department of Computer Science and Technology at Tsinghua University.

2006

Launch of AMiner

Released AMiner.org, an AI-driven academic search and mining platform, now connecting millions of researchers worldwide.

2014

Joint Research Center with [Partner Name]

Established the Tsinghua-[Partner] Joint Research Center for Big Data Mining, marking a new era of industrial collaboration.

2019

Cognitive Intelligence Key Lab

Became a core unit of the Tsinghua University Institute for Artificial Intelligence, focusing on Cognitive AI.

2023

ChatGLM & Commercialization

Successfully transferred ChatGLM technology to Zhipu AI (spin-off), accelerating the industrial application of Large Language Models.

2024

New Frontiers

Expanded research into Multi-modal Agents and embodied AI, establishing new benchmarks in the field.

Research Scope

Large Language Models

Foundation models (GLM series), efficient training, and reasoning capabilities.

Knowledge Graphs

Knowledge acquisition, reasoning, and neuro-symbolic integration.

Social Networks

Academic social network analysis, user modeling, and influence propagation.

Data Mining

Algorithms for massive datasets, pattern recognition, and predictive analytics.

Our Research Culture

Long-termism

We focus on fundamental, hard problems rather than low-hanging fruit. We believe in persistent effort over decades to achieve breakthroughs in AGI.

Openness

We are committed to Open Source. From ChatGLM to AMiner data, we share our models and datasets to democratize AI and foster global collaboration.

Theory + Systems

We pursue "Ding Tian Li Di" (顶天立地): aiming for world-class theoretical contributions while building robust, large-scale systems that work in the real world.

Join Our Community

We invite researchers, students, and innovators to collaborate with us on the exciting area of artificial general intelligence.
