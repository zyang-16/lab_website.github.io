The Tsinghua Knowledge Engineering Group (KEG) is dedicated to advancing the frontiers of Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) and empowering global academic discovery through AMiner.org.

Our Global Impact

We have developed a series of state-of-the-art models including GLM-130B, ChatGLM, CogView & CogVideo, and CodeGeex. Proudly, our pretrained base models have been adopted by over 1,000 organizations across 70+ countries. We also invented AMiner.org, connecting the global academic community.
