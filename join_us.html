Join Our Research Team

We are looking for highly-motivated students and researchers to work on the frontiers of AGI, Large Language Models, and Agentic Systems.

Who We Are Looking For

Students

For Undergrads, Master students, or visiting scholars.

  • Solid Math & Coding: Proficiency in Python and PyTorch is a must. Familiarity with Linux.
  • Self-Motivated: Passionate about AGI, especially in Code Agents & VLM.
  • Commitment: At least 6 months of internship time is preferred.
  • Bonus: Experience in NLP/CV competitions or open-source projects.
Python PyTorch VLM

Postdocs

For PhD graduates focusing on AGI theory & algorithms.

  • PhD Degree: In Computer Science, AI, or related fields.
  • Publication Record: At least 2 first-author papers in top-tier conferences (ACL, EACL, ICLR, NeurIPS, etc.).
  • Leadership: Ability to mentor junior students and lead research groups.
  • Research Vision: A clear research plan related to LLMs or Agents.
Top-tier Pubs Leadership

How to Apply

1. Prepare Your Materials

We need your CV/Resume and a Transcript (for students). If you have publications or a GitHub portfolio, please include them.

2. Choose Your Track

Specify if you are applying for a Student Internship, PhD Position, or Postdoctoral Fellow. Mention your specific research interests (e.g., Tool-use, Alignment, Multi-agent).

3. Send Email

Email your materials to keg.cs.tsinghua@gmail.com.
Please use the subject line:
[Application] Your Name - Position - University

4. Interview

If your profile matches our needs, we will contact you within 2 weeks to schedule an interview with our faculty or senior researchers.
