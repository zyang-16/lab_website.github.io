Students

Postdocs

For PhD graduates focusing on AGI theory & algorithms.

  • PhD Degree: In Computer Science, AI, or related fields.

Postdoctoral Fellow: AI for Science & Academic Big Data

Research Directions

  • Scientific Knowledge Graphs and Large-Scale Graph Learning: Leveraging AMiner expert profiling and paper citation networks to study how deep patterns of academic evolution can be extracted from structured scholarly data.
  • Domain-Specific Large Models and Agents for Scientific Research: Developing domain-specific agents capable of deep academic paper analysis, abstract summarization, and trend analysis.
  • Cross-Disciplinary Collaboration Modeling: Investigating algorithmic approaches to identify potential interdisciplinary collaboration opportunities, optimize global research network allocation, and enhance the efficiency of scientific discovery.

  • Ph.D. obtained within 3 years (or upcoming graduates), age < 35.
