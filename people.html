Xiao Liu 刘潇

PhD Student, 2021 - Present

Xiao Liu is a fifth-year PhD student in the Department of Computer Science at Tsinghua University. His primary research focuses on the algorithms and applications of foundation models and foundation agents. His main work includes the P-Tuning series on prompt tuning for large language models; the open-sourced 100B-parameter bilingual language model GLM-130B and the ChatGLM model series; AgentBench, a benchmark for evaluating LLM-based agents; and AutoGLM, the first LLM-based agent for phone usage. He has received honors including a nomination for the Best Paper Award at WWW 2022, Tsinghua University’s Special Class Scholarship (Graduate), and the National Scholarship.

Postdoctoral Researcher, 2023-2025

Wei Wang is an associate professor at Nankai University. Before that, he was a postdoc research fellow with Department of Computer Science and Technology, Tsinghua University from 2023 to 2025. He received the PhD degree at Tsinghua University in 2022, and received the bachelor degree at Yanshan University, in 2016. His research interests include artificial intelligence, natural language processing, and LLM evaluation. Recent work focuses on the application of LLM in education, data analysis, and game intelligence.
